FAQ (fast answers)

Is this “going ghost” / total anonymity?

No device can guarantee total anonymity. The goal here is practical privacy: reduce tracking, minimize data collection, and control what runs on your device.

Do I need to be technical to use these?

You don’t need to be an engineer. Like any platform shift, there’s a learning curve — but most people adapt quickly with basic guidance and good habits.

Will all my accessories and apps work?

Many will. Some specialized peripherals (especially older vendor-locked hardware) may require workarounds. If you rely on a specific tool, confirm compatibility first.

What’s the best starting device?

Start with the device you use most: for many, that’s the phone. If your work life is laptop-heavy, the Ghost Laptop is a strong first move.
